Navigating the website

Double clicking the edges, or the blank bottom area will bring you to the top of the website.

Triple clicking any h1 header will toggle the "debug stylesheet", which basically highlights every element. This will also give a feedback (vibration). To turn it off, simply triple click any h1 header.

Clicking text wrapped on a dark background like this, will copy text to your clipboard (feedback [vibration] is also available here too.).

Use the navigation bar at the bottom to navigate the website quickly. You can also use the top links (the arrows) to navigate around. Note: the back button uses your browser history to go back. (For the nerds: history.back())
