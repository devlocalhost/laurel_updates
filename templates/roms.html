Android Versions

Recommended versions are android 11 and 13, because these are the ones usually getting updates. Android 10 and 9 are not included because no one is interested, plus most laurel users are using "android 11 fw", which enables anti-rollback, preventing flashing anything lower than android 11 (will result in a brick).
