What is RDP?

Expand

RDP is a way to implement dynamic partitions on old devices. It works by merging normal device partitions into one super partition, and then creating logical partitions on that super partition as needed. This allows developers/maintainers to use unused space in normal ROMs to add extra space to partitions that need it. You cannot use an RDP kernel on a ROM that doesnt use RDP partitions, and a non RDP kernel on a RDP ROM.
